Abandoned Ballparks

This is a complete list of all ballparks abandoned by major league teams and their minor league affiliates from 1999 through 2015. Some have since been demolished, others are still standing but vacant, and a handful are now used by independent or collegiate teams. The table below lists the most generally recognized name of the ballpark, its location, the year it opened and was abandoned, and its current status.

List compiled and updated by Graham Knight
