About Us

Throughout Joe Mock's three-decade career in the health-insurance field, his philosophy of servicing clients has never changed: let nothing stand in the way of getting the question answered or the problem fixed. Joe applied that principle when he formed All American Resource Group in 2013, and it continues today at On Target Insurance Services.

Each team member at On Target pledges to uphold the company’s commitment to delivering second-to-none service levels to our clients and their personnel. And when those employees or their family members need assistance with coverages like life insurance or Christian healthcare cost-sharing ministries, we can help there, too.

Joe Mock

Owner

Joe entered the group health insurance field in 1986. Since then, he has worked for two carriers and three insurance agencies, before forming On Target Insurance Services in 2019. With Cigna, he specialized in fully-insured health coverage for middle-market and larger accounts. With Entrust, he worked primarily on self-insured medical programs for groups of all sizes. While employed at various insurance agencies, he applied what he’d learned while at the insurers to support the employer groups he continues to work with to this day.

“I wouldn’t trade the 14 years I spent with carriers for anything,” Mock pointed out. “I’m a more effective advocate for my clients when solving their problems and negotiating with insurers and TPAs because I speak their language.” Further, because he worked for both a large insurer and a TPA, his knowledge of self-insured plans is as solid as his experience with traditional insurance.

He offers special expertise for auto dealerships, as he implemented and serviced benefit plans for dealerships under the Ford Blue Oval Healthcare Initiative for much of the past decade. “I bet I’ve conducted more enrollment meetings at auto dealerships than anyone in America,” he says with pride.

Joe holds two degrees from West Virginia University, and has served as the President of the local WVU alumni chapter in Texas. He is licensed for Life and Health in Arkansas, Arizona, California, Colorado, Indiana, Kansas, Louisiana, Missouri, Oklahoma, Tennessee, Texas and West Virginia.

Timothy Aydelott

Account Executive

Timothy joined AARG in April of 2018, and continues with On Target today. He has a dual role at our agency, being responsible for client support and new-business development.

He brings over 25 years of experience serving customers to On Target, including eight in management and five in sales. "I've had daily dealings with many customers and clients in my past positions," says Timothy. "I've always tried to go above and beyond the job description for them. I do my best to build great rapport with every employer and their employees, as I spend a little extra time getting to know them and their needs."

Timothy holds a Life and Health License in Texas, as well as national certifications in the firearms industry. Indeed, he is “on target” with our clients on their employee-benefits needs just as he is when he is a firearms instructor.

Dave DeNure

Co-Founder

In 1993, Dave began his career in insurance with CBHP/Principal/NYLCare/Aetna, where he dealt with all manner of employee benefit coverages -- both fully insured and self insured. He has also served in leadership roles at two agencies before joining forces with Joe Mock to create AARG in 2013.

While his knowledge is extensive in all aspects of group insurance, he is particularly experienced in self-funded health plans for employers. He has designed many such money-saving programs, including Minimum Essential Coverage (MEC) plans.

"Those who know me are always saying that it's obvious what matters the most to me: taking care of our clients no matter what," Dave explained. "It doesn't matter if the person is the president of the company or someone who was just hired, I try to show them respect and resolve their issue very quickly."

Dave proudly served our country in the U.S. Navy in the Gulf War and is a Disabled Veteran. He is licensed for Life & Health, Property & Casualty, and Life Counselor in Texas, and is certified in all areas of PPACA.
