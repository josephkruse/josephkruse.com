• What is done after your information has been collected by OTIS

When providing you with a quote or processing your application, we may contact you using the personal information you provided us.

We may contact you to survey your satisfaction with our services as well as inform you about additional products and services. You have the ability to opt out of these surveys and/or notices by contacting us by email using the Contact page of our site.

You may continue to receive communications from us in regards to your insurance quote, application or policy even if you make the decision to opt out of receiving notices of additional products and services.

Here at OTIS, we do not disclose your personal information to third parties, unless there are obligations regarding insurance companies, licensed agents or legal matters. If you submit an application for an insurance product offered by us, then we would disclose/forward your personal information to your selected insurance company for processing. If you were to submit an application for an insurance product offered through an insurance agent or agency with whom we are associated, then we may disclose your personal information to that agent or agency to process your requested quote or application. The agent or agency is only permitted to use your personal information to process your requested quote or application and is contractually obligated to maintain strict confidentiality. Other than these exceptions, we will not share your personal information with third parties.

OTIS will keep your information long enough for the specified objective to be achieved for which the information was collected, or as long as specified in the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act.

To keep your personal information accurate and up to date, OTIS continues to evaluate our efforts. If you want to correct or update your contact information, you may contact us using the Contact page of our site. If your application has already been submitted to your health insurance company of choice, you may have to contact them directly to update your information.

Should you choose not to provide us with your personal information, it could prevent us from being able to provide you with an insurance quote.

• Filing a complaint if you feel your rights are violated

You can complain if you feel we have violated your privacy rights by using the contact information at the end of this notice. You can also file a complaint with the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services Office for Civil Rights by calling 877-696-6775; or by visiting their website www.hhs.gov/ocr/privacy/hipaa/complaints. We will not retaliate against you for filing a complaint.

For more information about your privacy policy rights please go to: www.hhs.gov/hipaa/for-professionals/privacy/laws-regulations/index.html.
