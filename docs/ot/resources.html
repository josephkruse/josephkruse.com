Resources

We urge our clients and their employees to come to us for all of their issues with health care reform, insurance companies, prescription benefits, ancillary coverages, etc. However, feel free to explore some of our favorite resources and news sources:

Healthcare Reform

Straight from the horse’s mouth, so to speak, here is the Federal Government’s take on the Affordable Care Act.
www.hhs.gov/healthcare/rights/

Where To Look

Another site from the Feds, this one directs visitors to public resources for various illnesses and circumstances.
www.hrsa.gov

Healthy Advice

The Harvard Medical School provides a site with numerous articles on healthy living and identifying symptoms.
www.health.harvard.edu

For What Ails You

Tried and true, WebMD provides a wealth of of information on various ailments and conditions.
www.webmd.com/

For Healthy Families

Sponsored by the American Academy of Family Physicians, this site provides insight into a number of illnesses.
www.familydoctor.org

Prevention Central

This site, operated by the U.S. Dept. of Health & Human Services, shows you what preventive care you may need.
www.healthfinder.gov
